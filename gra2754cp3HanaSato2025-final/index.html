Strawberry Red Bean Mochi

Directions

1. Prepare the strawberries

Wash, dry, and hull the strawberries.

2. Wrap the strawberries with red bean paste

Roll the red bean paste into balls. Flatten the ball and place a strawberry in the center. Then, wrap the strawberry with the red bean paste.

3. Make the mochi

In a microwave-safe bowl, whisk together ¾ cup rice flour, 1 ½ tbsp granulated sugar, 150 ml water. Loosely wrap the bowl with plastic wrap and microwave for 1 minute.

4. Stir the mochi

Moisten your silicone spatula with room-temperature water. Stir the mixture and microwave for another 1 minute. Stir again, and microwave for 30-second intervals until the dough becomes thick and slightly translucent.

5. Shape the mochi

Dust the surface with potato starch and place the mochi on top. Use a rolling pin to roll out the mochi into a ⅛-inch thin dough. Cut the dough into 3-4 inch squares. Place the prepared strawberry in the center. Cover the strawberry with the mochi, pinching the ends together to form a ball.

6. Final touch

Cut the strawberry red bean mochi in half and enjoy!
